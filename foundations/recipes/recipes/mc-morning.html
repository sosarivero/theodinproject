Breakfast: McMorning

Ingredients

  • 4x Breakfast Sandwiches
  • 4x Hash Browns
  • 2x packs of mayonnaise.

Steps

  1. Order the sandwiches.
  2. Order the hash browns.
  3. Grab the mayonnaise.
  4. Put mayonnaise on hash browns.
  5. Slip them into the sandwiches.
  6. Squish sandwiches down.
  7. Eat them.
