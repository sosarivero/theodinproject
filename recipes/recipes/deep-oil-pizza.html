Deep Oil Pizza

Ingredients

  • A functioning phone.
  • Phone number of Pizza delivery.
  • Olive oil, canola oil, whatever.
  • Not motor oil.
  • A coffee table.
  • Power of will

Steps

  1. Order an extra-large pizza with everything on.
  2. If you don't like sardines, don't put 'em on.
  3. Pay the delivery guy.
  4. Take the pizza to the coffee table.
  5. Open that fucker up.
  6. Grab the bottle of your preferred oil (not motor oil).
  7. Pour it over the pizza until half of the bottle is gone. Soak it.
  8. Intensely stare at the pizza.
  9. Understand that that pizza is keeping you from your goals.
